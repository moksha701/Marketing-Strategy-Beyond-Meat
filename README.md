# Beyond Meat — Digital Marketing Strategy

**Simulated Engagement:** Deloitte Digital (Consultant Group)
**Budget:** $10,000,000 | **Target Audience:** 18–44 year-olds

---

## Purpose of the Project

Developed a $10M digital-first marketing strategy for Beyond Meat as a simulated
Deloitte Digital consulting engagement. The goal was to rebuild brand trust, improve
market share, and drive first-time and repeat purchases in a declining plant-based
meat category.

Main objectives:
* Identify the most effective digital channels to reach 18–44 year-old consumers
* Develop data-driven tactics to reverse declining sales trends
* Create campaign assets including banner ads, SEM campaigns, and social content

---

## Strategy Framework — 4P's

| Area | Strategy |
|------|----------|
| **Product** | Emphasize taste, quality, and ingredient transparency (pea protein, non-GMO) |
| **Price** | Wholesale value bundles, volume-based discounts for bulk buyers |
| **Place** | E-commerce (Amazon Fresh, Instacart, Walmart.com) + restaurant partnerships |
| **Promotion** | TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts + influencer and paid media |

## Target Personas

**Charley Cohen** — Age 36, NJ. Health-conscious father seeking quick, nutritious
family meals. Influenced by podcasts and YouTube reviews.

**Elisa Rodriguez** — Age 41, NJ. Wellness-focused small business owner who values
ingredient transparency and sustainability. Reached via email and Instagram.

**Emily Torres** — Age 24, Brooklyn. Gen Z trendsetter driven by TikTok creators
and peer reviews. Budget-conscious, values authenticity.

---

## Key Recommendations

1. Expand restaurant collaborations with aligned partners (Moe's, Shake Shack, Chick-fil-A)
2. Rebuild trust via ingredient transparency campaign: "What's In Beyond?"
3. Boost trial with value bundles and promo codes via HelloFresh, Trader Joe's, DoorDash
4. Partner with micro/mid-tier influencers for authentic taste-test content
5. Create seasonal limited-edition launches to drive FOMO and repeat engagement
